A Sanctuary for Gastronomic Connoisseurs

INFORMATION & POLICIES

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the restaurant dress code?

We enforce a Smart Elegant dress code. Jackets are encouraged for gentlemen; athletic wear, beachwear, and flip-flops are strictly prohibited to maintain an atmosphere of refined luxury.

Can you accommodate severe dietary allergies or vegan preferences?

Absolutely. Chef Antoine and our team craft specialized 7-course Vegan and Gluten-Free tasting menus. Please notify us of severe allergies at least 24 hours prior to your reservation.

Is valet parking available on-site?

Complimentary luxury white-glove valet parking is provided at the main porte-cochère entrance on Celestial Avenue.

How far in advance should I book for weekend dining?

We recommend booking 2 to 4 weeks in advance for Friday and Saturday evening seatings. Private VIP Salon bookings open 60 days in advance.
